Contribute to Science

#mind #tech

There are many ways that you can contribute from home.

Esc
Contribute to Science

One way in which you can make a contribution is by labelling. You can help mozilla to teach robots how to speak or openstreetmaps by helping them label your neighborhood. You can also help universities in general via the zooniverse project.


Another way to help is to offer your computers resources to perform complicated computations. Projects like folding at home allow you to help with calculating how protein structures fold (which is helping the fight against many diseases like corona).

Esc
Pomodoro

Pomodoro is a method of working in 25 minute segments.


It makes you aware of the time in a day and it prevents you from getting stuck in a rabbit hole. There's a great video on youtube and a small course can be found here.


There's great apps that you can download but the most popular one seems to be tomighty. If you're interested in knowing more behind the theory behind it you might appreciate reading the book written by the creator.

Alternatively, you can use this free website to set a timer.


If possible, consider buying the book from a local book-store.

Gift a Stranger
